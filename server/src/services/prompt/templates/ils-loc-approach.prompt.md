## Procedure-Specific Instructions

The current task is ILS/LOC Approach recognition.

Focus on:
- IAF, IF, FAF, MAPt, LOC frequency, GP/DME, final approach course, altitude constraints, missed approach, and minima
- runway data, landing aids, lighting, and navaid details
- stepdown fixes and procedure variants such as ILS, LOC, Y/Z/X/W variants

Do not mix minima with procedure leg sequence. Minima belong in the minima output area, not as route legs.
